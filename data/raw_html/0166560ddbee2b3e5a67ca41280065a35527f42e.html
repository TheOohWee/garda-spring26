V116 was shown to be immunogenic for all 21 serotypes covered by the vaccine, including a pneumococcal vaccine-naive and vaccine-experience adults, as well as those at increased risk for pneumococcal disease. First-in-human studies are scheduled to start in the fourth quarter of this year. The clinical trials are anticipated to start enrolling in the fourth quarter.
