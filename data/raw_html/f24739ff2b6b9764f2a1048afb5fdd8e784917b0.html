Earnings Call Transcript

GameSquare (GAME) Q2 2025 Earnings Call Transcript

By Motley Fool Transcribing – Apr 8, 2026 at 2:34PM EST

Image source: The Motley Fool.

DATE

Thursday, Aug. 14, 2025 at 5 p.m. ET

CALL PARTICIPANTS

  • Chief Executive Officer — Justin Kenna
  • Chief Financial Officer — Michael Munoz

TAKEAWAYS

  • Consolidated Revenue -- $15.9 million, reflecting an 11% decrease due to lower programmatic advertising, partially offset by other segment growth.
  • Gross Margin -- $2.4 million or 15.3% of sales, up 120 basis points from the prior year, with management expecting further margin improvements.
  • Adjusted EBITDA Loss -- $3.5 million, an improvement of $0.7 million year over year and $0.5 million sequentially from Q1 2025.
  • SG&A Reduction -- Expenses decreased by 15%, contributing to year over year adjusted EBITDA improvement.
  • Restructuring Savings -- Additional $5 million in annualized savings identified, expected to contribute starting in Q3.
  • Operating Revenue Mix -- Programmatic advertising decline noted as low-margin, with future focus on higher-margin agency, gaming, and technology services.
  • Divestiture -- Sold remaining 25.5% stake in FaZe Media for a valuation above $39 million; original combined FaZe acquisition was $14 million in stock.
  • Onchain Treasury Assets -- 15,630 ETH held, original cost basis $55 million, current market value $74.3 million, plus digital assets including one Ape CryptoPunk NFT purchased for $5.15 million (1,084 ETH).
  • Treasury Unrealized Gains -- Over $19 million in unrealized gains reported since the launch of the onchain strategy in July.
  • Targeted Onchain Yields -- Ethereum yield strategy commenced Aug. 1, targeting 8%-14% annualized returns; NFT yield strategy targeting 6%-10% annualized stablecoin yields.
  • Cash, Digital Assets, and Debt -- $20 million in cash and equivalents, $79.4 million in onchain treasury assets, and $1.25 million in total debt as of Aug. 13, 2025.
  • Share Repurchase Program -- Board approved up to $5 million in buybacks, to be funded from onchain yield strategy proceeds.
  • Key Client Win -- New agency of record deal with Azuki for $2.5 million, considered high-margin, recognized in the back half of the year.
  • Crypto-Native Business Development -- Over 15 active discussions with crypto-native organizations, representing potential 8-figure deal value.
  • Operating Platform Focus -- SaaS/managed services, agency/media, owned IP, and FaZe Clan esports are the four operational priorities.
  • Product Launches and Partnerships -- Stream Hatchet secured its largest contract for Monster Hunter Wilds and announced new engagement with Ubisoft; proprietary AI-based solution for influencer marketing launched.
  • Outlook on Revenue Seasonality -- Management expects approximately 60% of annual revenue will be generated in the second half, with Q3 and Q4 sequential growth anticipated.

Need a quote from a Motley Fool analyst? Email [email protected]

RISKS

  • Revenue Shortfall -- Management acknowledged, "consolidated revenue in the second quarter was slightly below expectation due to extended closing time lines of several last deals."
  • Macroeconomic and Trade-Related Delays -- Tariff-related uncertainty delayed multi-year, seven-figure deals with global gaming companies headquartered in China.
  • Programmatic Advertising Weakness -- CFO Munoz noted, "The 11% year-over-year decline in revenue was primarily due to a reduction in programmatic advertising revenue."

SUMMARY

GameSquare Holdings (GAME +14.66%) reported a year-over-year revenue decline in the second quarter but highlighted substantial gains from its newly launched onchain treasury strategy. Management stated that treasury investments generated over $19 million in unrealized gains and set the foundation for new high-margin revenue streams through exclusive partnerships and agency wins. Leadership emphasized robust operating discipline, $5 million in annualized restructuring savings, and a strengthened focus on higher-margin segments, positioning Q3 and Q4 for sequential revenue growth. A board-approved $5 million share repurchase program will be funded directly by onchain yield results, demonstrating a commitment to direct shareholder returns from crypto activities.

  • Over 15 active crypto-native business development conversations may yield additional eight-figure deals, broadening GameSquare’s revenue diversification.
  • Stream Hatchet’s largest contract to date, and the launch of proprietary AI-based tools, indicate ongoing investment in next-generation marketing capabilities.
  • The recently closed FaZe Media divestiture reduced complexity and released working capital, simplifying the business for further scalability.
  • The company’s net asset value and liquidity ($99.4 million in ETH, NFTs, and cash vs. $1.25 million debt) enable flexibility for future investment and capital return strategies.

INDUSTRY GLOSSARY

  • ETH: Abbreviation for Ethereum, a leading blockchain platform supporting decentralized applications and digital assets.
  • Onchain Treasury Strategy: A corporate strategy involving holding and actively managing blockchain-based assets, such as cryptocurrencies and NFTs, to generate yield and appreciation.
  • Programmatic Advertising: Automated digital advertising typically sold via software, often regarded as low-margin in media businesses.
  • Stablecoin: A type of cryptocurrency pegged to the value of a stable asset, typically used for yield strategies in digital asset management.
  • Agency of Record: A formal client engagement in which the agency becomes the primary provider of marketing or creative services.
