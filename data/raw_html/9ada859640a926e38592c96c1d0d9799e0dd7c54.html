TAKEAWAYS

  • Total Revenue -- $7.7 billion, increasing 9% year over year, with both Moody's Investors Service (MIS) and Moody's Analytics (MA) up 9% each.
  • Adjusted Operating Margin -- 51.1%, expanding by 300 basis points year over year, reflecting greater operating leverage.
  • Adjusted Diluted EPS -- $14.94, up 20% year over year, and a 70% earnings increase over three years, with management highlighting "a 20% CAGR since 2022."
  • Debt Rated -- $6.6 trillion for the year, an all-time high, supporting infrastructure, AI-driven data centers, energy transition finance, and private credit sectors.
  • Private Credit Revenue -- Up 60% in 2025, with Moody's being the sole rating agency on the largest private credit CLO of the year, valued at $1.5 billion.
  • Moody's Analytics Recurring Revenue -- Grew 11% and represented 97% of MA's fourth quarter revenue, indicating strong recurring business.
  • CreditLens Growth -- Revenue from CreditLens approached 20% growth, with two-thirds of eligible renewals converting to the AI-enabled lending suite, generating an average uplift of approximately 67%.
  • Lending Suite Adoption -- Tier one U.S. bank deployment of AgenTix generated approximately 35%-40% of each credit memo and claimed "millions of dollars saved," according to customer feedback.
  • KYC ARR Growth -- 15% in 2025, with steady mid- to high-teens growth over two years, driven by deeper penetration in banking and expansion to non-financial corporates.
  • Insurance ARR Growth -- 7% year over year, up 21% over two years, propelled by customer migration to Intelligent Risk Platform and new high-definition model adoption.
  • MA Adjusted Operating Margin -- 33.1%, rising 240 basis points year over year and outpacing acquisition-related headwinds.
  • Research & Insights ARR -- Grew 8%, supported by enhanced CreditView features and expansion of agentic solutions for workflow integration.
  • Data & Information ARR -- 7% annual growth, with ratings data feeds growing above segment average, and notable enterprise wins offsetting U.S. government cancellations.
  • MIS Revenue (Q4) -- Rose 17% year over year, with transactional revenue up 22% on 10% issuance growth and a more favorable deal mix.
  • MIS Adjusted Operating Margin -- 63.6% for the full year, improving 350 basis points year over year due to operating leverage and technology investment.
  • 2026 Guidance: MIS -- Revenue expected to grow at a high single-digit percent pace, with projected operating margin of about 65%, up 150 basis points versus 2025.
  • 2026 Guidance: MA -- Organic constant currency recurring revenue expected to grow high single-digit percent, adjusted operating margin projected at 34%-35%, and reported revenue at the high end of mid-single-digit percent growth, including a 180 basis point divestiture headwind.
  • 2026 Adjusted Diluted EPS Guidance -- $16.40 to $17.00, reflecting approximately 12% projected growth at the midpoint.
  • 2026 Free Cash Flow Guidance -- Between $2.8 billion and $3.0 billion, a 13% growth at midpoint, with $100 million CapEx increase for office build-outs.
  • Capital Returns -- Company targets a return of at least 90% of free cash flow to shareholders in 2026, comprising $2.0 billion in share repurchases and a 10% dividend increase.
  • Divestitures Impact -- Learning Solutions divestiture is a roughly one percentage point headwind to MCO revenue growth and a two percentage point headwind to MA's reported revenue growth for 2026, with minor margin dilution.
  • Customer Cohort Growth -- Strategic customers purchasing GenAI or AgenTix solutions retained at 97% rate and exhibited growth at roughly double the rate versus the rest of MA's base.
  • ARR Excludes Divestitures -- Both ARR and organic recurring revenue metrics are adjusted to eliminate the influence of divestitures and acquisitions.
  • Effective Tax Rate (2026) -- Expected in the 23%-25% range; a return to a normalized rate following a significant one-time M&A tax benefit in 2025.

SUMMARY

Moody's Corporation (MCO 2.41%) reported record results with broad-based revenue growth, margin expansion, and high recurring revenue, while the company positioned itself as a key provider of decision-grade data and analytics embedded in AI-driven workflows. Management highlighted surging demand for ratings in private credit and infrastructure, cited increasing adoption of AI-enabled solutions by the largest institutional customers, and described robust market adoption of advanced KYC and compliance offerings. The company outlined a transition to a more focused analytics portfolio via recent and ongoing divestitures, while establishing dedicated teams to accelerate growth in agent-ready data, lending, and insurance intelligence. Guidance for 2026 indicated high single-digit percent revenue growth for MIS, persistent high ARR growth for MA, and aggressive capital return plans targeting over 90% of free cash flow to shareholders.

  • Management stated, "our strongest growth came from our largest strategic customers," indicating durable expansion in high-value client relationships across both ratings and analytics.
  • The company emphasized its proprietary "trusted context layer" that "sits between the raw data assets and the AI reasoning engines," citing it as a major differentiator in AI adoption.
  • A tier one U.S. bank using Moody's AgenTix reported savings in the "millions of dollars," with real-time risk monitoring now enabled via API integration.
  • Noémie Heuland noted a "one percentage point headwind to MCO revenue growth" from the Learning Solutions divestiture, but forecast modest long-term improvement in revenue growth rates as low-growth components roll off.
  • In the insurance vertical, cloud-based model adoption and high-definition data products produced a 21% two-year ARR uptick, aligned with emerging climate risk trends.
  • Leadership described the move toward consumption-based pricing models, positioning Moody's to participate in value capture from increased customer efficiency due to AI and automation.

INDUSTRY GLOSSARY

  • CLO (Collateralized Loan Obligation): A single security backed by a pool of debt, typically low-rated corporate loans, where pooled cash flows are distributed in tranches to investors.
  • ARR (Annualized Recurring Revenue): Indicator of forward-looking recurring revenue generated from active contracts, normalized for currency and M&A activity, and used as a key growth metric in software and analytics businesses.
  • KYC (Know Your Customer): Regulatory and compliance set of processes to identify and verify client identities, primarily to prevent financial crimes.
  • Orbis: A Moody's proprietary database containing global company information, legal ownership, people, and firmographic data, curated for use in risk, compliance, and analytical applications.
  • Agentic Solutions / AgenTix: Moody's AI-enabled workflow tools and specialized agents designed to automate credit memo creation and risk monitoring in client operations.
  • Intelligent Risk Platform (IRP): Moody's cloud-based analytics suite for insurance clients, supporting advanced modeling, underwriting, and risk analysis through integrated proprietary datasets.
