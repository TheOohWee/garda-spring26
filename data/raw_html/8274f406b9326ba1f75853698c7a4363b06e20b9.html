RISKS

  • Chief Financial Officer Alan Kirshenbaum said, "as it stands now, we're behind our Investor Day goals," citing headwinds in private credit, AI and software, slower non-traded BDC and private wealth flows, and increased tender activity in non-traded BDCs.
  • Institutional fundraising timelines have nearly doubled to more than two years for real estate funds, and about half of those funds fell short of fundraising targets, which management called broader challenges in this asset class.
  • Non-traded BDCs saw industry-wide capital raising slowdowns and elevated redemptions in Q4, attributed by management to prior market environments with heightened volatility and fear.

SUMMARY

Blue Owl Capital Inc. (OWL 0.66%) reported substantial annual fundraising and AUM milestones, with the firm surpassing $300 billion in assets and achieving a record $56 billion in capital raised for 2025. Management detailed strong investment performance across strategies, including net lease and tech lending, with notable outperformance in key products versus benchmarks. The earnings call confirmed expansion in both private wealth and institutional channels, adding scale and diversification through new evergreen products, global partnerships, and targeted acquisitions. Leadership highlighted proactive margin management and capital return initiatives, while signaling modest per-share FRE growth rate increases in 2026 and acceleration potential in 2027 amid ongoing macro uncertainty.

  • Chief Financial Officer Alan Kirshenbaum indicated that management expects to continue modestly raising dividends while working to lower the payout ratio in coming years.
  • The firm’s AUM not yet paying fees represents substantial embedded future management fee growth as those assets are deployed.
  • Blue Owl’s technological and sectoral diversification was emphasized, with management stating, "30% of our credit business is away from direct lending," and further expansion outside direct lending underway.
  • Chief Executive Officer Marc Lipschultz stated, "answer, no. We don't have red flags. In point of fact, we don't have yellow flags. We actually have largely green flags," directly addressing market concerns about tech and software portfolios.

INDUSTRY GLOSSARY

  • OREP: Blue Owl Real Estate Platform, the firm’s non-traded REIT focused on generating private real estate returns.
  • BDCs: Business Development Companies, regulated investment vehicles focused on lending to middle-market companies.
  • FRE: Fee-Related Earnings, a non-GAAP measure of asset managers’ recurring fee-based profits, excluding performance fees and investment income.
  • DE: Distributable Earnings, a measure reflecting the cash generated for potential distribution to shareholders.
  • Net Lease: Real estate investment strategy focusing on triple net leases, passing property expenses to tenants while seeking steady income streams.
  • GP Stakes: Minority investments in private asset manager general partnerships, providing long-term exposure to management fee streams.
  • LTV: Loan-to-Value, a credit metric expressing loan principal as a percentage of the underlying asset’s appraised value.
