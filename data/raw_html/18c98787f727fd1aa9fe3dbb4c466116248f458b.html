  • Non-dilutive financing sources -- Potential future cash sources include the sale of a priority review voucher, program milestones, and additional funds from healthcare royalty agreements.

Industry glossary

  • BLA (Biologics License Application): Regulatory submission seeking marketing authorization for a biologic product in the United States.
  • PDUFA date: The scheduled decision date by which the FDA commits to review a new drug or biologic application.
  • PPQ (Process Performance Qualification): The stage in manufacturing validation that demonstrates production consistency and readiness for commercial supply.
  • SAE (Serious Adverse Event): Any untoward medical occurrence that results in death, is life-threatening, requires hospitalization, or causes significant disability.
  • NSAA (North Star Ambulatory Assessment): A clinical assessment of motor function in ambulatory Duchenne muscular dystrophy patients.
  • DRSS (Diabetic Retinopathy Severity Scale): Ordinal scale measuring severity changes in diabetic retinopathy used for primary endpoints in clinical trials.
  • CTAP (Critical Therapeutic Assessment Program) disease progression model: An external control model referenced in functional outcome evaluations for Duchenne clinical studies.
  • PRV (Priority Review Voucher): Transferable FDA-issued incentive that awards expedited review timelines to a future drug or biologic application.
  • NPDR (Nonproliferative Diabetic Retinopathy): An early stage of diabetic retinopathy assessed in clinical trials of ophthalmologic therapies.
