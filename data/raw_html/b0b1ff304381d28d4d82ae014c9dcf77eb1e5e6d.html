Bridgeline Digital (BLIN) Earnings Transcript

By Motley Fool Transcribing – Jan 6, 2026 at 1:52PM EST

Image source: The Motley Fool.

DATE

Thursday, December 18, 2025 at 4:30 p.m. ET

CALL PARTICIPANTS

  • President and Chief Executive Officer — Roger Kahn
  • Chief Financial Officer — Thomas Windhausen

Need a quote from a Motley Fool analyst? Email [email protected]

TAKEAWAYS

  • HawkSearch Revenue -- $8.9 million, representing 58% of total revenue and over 60% of subscription revenue, with a 75% gross margin.
  • Gartner Ranking -- HawkSearch named #1 for B2B search use cases in the 2025 Critical Capabilities report.
  • Net Revenue Retention -- 117%, reflecting less than 4% churn and significant license upgrades among core product customers.
  • HawkSearch Sales Metrics -- 83 HawkSearch licenses sold with $6.9 million total contract value and $2.4 million ARR, representing an 18% increase over the prior year.
  • Sales Pipeline -- Ended the quarter with a 65% larger sales pipeline, shorter sales cycle, and higher average sales size.
  • Sales Cycle and ARR -- HawkSearch's average sales cycle reduced from 160 days to 92 days, with average ARR per sale increasing 35% from $18,500 to $25,000.
  • Customer Expansions -- Existing customers such as Techo-Bloc, Do it Best, and a national distributor implemented AI products, with the national distributor experiencing 5x greater homepage revenue and 7x more revenue on their checkout screen.
  • New Customers Added -- 28 new logo accounts contributed $2 million in total contract value and $700,000 in ARR.
  • Subscription Revenue -- $3.1 million, or 81% of total revenue for the quarter; services revenue was $700,000, representing 19%.
  • Gross Profit and Margin -- $2.5 million gross profit and a 66% overall gross margin; subscription gross margin was 69%, services margin 50%.
  • Operating Expenses -- $3 million, down from $3.1 million in the year-ago period.
  • Net Loss -- $400,000, unchanged from the previous year period.
  • Adjusted EBITDA (non-GAAP) -- Negative $169,000, compared to $5,000 positive in the prior year comparable period.
  • Cash Balance -- $1.6 million cash and $1.5 million accounts receivable as of September 30, 2025.
  • Debt -- EUR 278,000 (about USD 326,000) at a 3.4% average interest rate, with principal payments due evenly through 2028; no other debt or outstanding earn-outs.
  • Sales and Marketing Spend -- $1.1 million for the September quarter, with $500,000 per quarter allocated to ad spend.
  • Partnerships -- Announced new B2B platform partnership with Unilog; HawkSearch available on Salesforce AppExchange, set to influence future revenues.
  • Product Innovation -- Six AI-powered products launched in the year, spanning Smart Search, Smart Response, Smart Agents, multisite management, Rapid UI, and advanced analytics.
  • Outstanding Shares and Warrants -- 12.2 million shares, 852,000 warrants (including 167,000 at $2.85 expiring May 2026 and 592,000 at $2.51 expiring November 2026), and just under 2 million stock options outstanding.

SUMMARY

Bridgeline Digital (BLIN +3.24%) emphasized core revenue growth driven by HawkSearch, underlined by renewed customer investment and recognition as an industry leader by Gartner. Management introduced quarterly reporting for ARR metrics, signaling the centrality of core products and anticipated dominance of HawkSearch in future financials. The call highlighted a marked increase in sales efficiency and sales pipeline, with new platform partnerships broadening total addressable market and setting the stage for incremental revenue contributions from fiscal 2026 onward.

  • Roger Kahn said, "Our expectation is that as we go through 2026 that, that is going to dominate our overall financials and the characteristics that we're seeing right now on that core component itself will be the characteristics that we have in the long term for the business as a whole."
  • Salesforce AppExchange and Unilog integrations are positioned as early-stage contributors, with meaningful revenue impact expected to scale in future quarters.
  • HawkSearch's low churn, increased up-sell, and expanded initial customer buy-in were cited as enabling a more predictable and higher-quality recurring revenue profile.

INDUSTRY GLOSSARY

  • Net Revenue Retention (NRR): Measures percentage of recurring revenue retained from existing customers over a period, including upgrades, downgrades, and churn.
  • Total Addressable Market (TAM): The overall revenue opportunity available from all potential customers for a product or service.
  • ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue): The value of recurring subscription revenues normalized to a one-year period.
  • Smart Agents: AI-powered tools for automated integration and third-party workflows within HawkSearch.
  • Rapid UI: User interface solution designed to reduce customer implementation effort and extend market reach to mid-market and SMB segments.
