We added 3.6 million smokeless consumers, reaching 29.1 million, mainly driven by our continued success in Modern Oral. Our focus on quality growth, balancing top and bottom line delivery, has driven a further improvement in new category contribution, up 251 million pounds, and a 7 percentage point increase in our category contribution margin on an organic constant rates basis.

We are committed to rewarding shareholders with strong cash returns, and I'm pleased with our progress improving financial flexibility. Enabling the initiation of sustainable share buyback, continued progress on the leverage to within our target range at 2.4 times, alongside our progressive dividend with 2% growth announced today.
