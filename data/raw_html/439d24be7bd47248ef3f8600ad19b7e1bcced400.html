For any given level of risk, we have been able to offer larger loans, and we've been able to offer lower interest rates, better pricing, to our customers over the course of 2024 through, as I mentioned, getting to know our customers better from a personal lending perspective and also through significant investments in credit modeling, which have yielded really significant results, and we expect that to continue through this year as well. And the third thing I would mention is that similar to what I talked about in credit card, where the bulk of our book was in the mass market historically, we are now starting to expand personal lending and adjust the product to be a better fit for new segments.
