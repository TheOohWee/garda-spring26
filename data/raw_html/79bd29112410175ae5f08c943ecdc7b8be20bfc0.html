Investors can find a summary of the impacts on our financials in this quarter, shareholders letter, and a full reconciliation in our earnings presentation. Consolidated revenues grew at a fast pace on the back of the strong operational momentum that I mentioned earlier. Brazil and Mexico had an outstanding quarter, and the revenues growth was sufficient to offset the impact of headwinds in Argentina.

Income from operations grew strongly year on year, once again, with margin expansion being driven by Mexico and Brazil. And our long-term ambition is to continue delivering both growth and profit, and we are confident in our ability to achieve that. We look forward to the next 25 years with great confidence and optimism as we still see plenty of opportunities to continue to grow and fulfill our mission in a region that provides us with a large addressable market.
