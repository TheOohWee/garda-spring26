We continue to expect to generate approximately $10.5 billion of free cash flow for 2022, through approximately $12 billion in cash from operations, less about $1.5 million in capital investments. Lastly, given the backdrop of rising interest rates, we expect to see an impact on our interest expense given our effective exposure to floating rate debt. We are encouraged by the momentum in our top line and we leverage our capabilities to sustain top-line growth with the ongoing inflationary backdrop.
