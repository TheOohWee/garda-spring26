Furthermore, regulatory reforms, in particular, the 24-hour slice-of-day construct in California, have solidified the value of our gas assets for load-serving entities to achieve compliance with that reform. Having established the contracted position we have now into 2027, we will be disciplined about pacing our remaining contracting of RA capacity for 2027 to 2030, being focused on aligning with the state's load-serving entities around the full value that the planned ore capacity is likely to convey in this new regulatory constructs.
