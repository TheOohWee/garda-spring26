Adjusted EBITDA was $300.1 million or 30.2% of revenue for the third quarter, compared to $314.5 million or 32.3% of revenue for the third quarter of 2023. Net income for the third quarter was $179.9 million or $0.45 per share.

We remain in a great financial position, closing the quarter with approximately $2.5 billion of cash and cash equivalents.
