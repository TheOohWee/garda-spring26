As Health Canada's regulations prevent dialogue and education around cannabis products, the consumer is left with fewer tools to make educated decisions about which products best suit them. The result is that price and THC content have become their paramount decision drivers when purchasing flower, which has led LPs to race to the bottom on price with many falsely overstating the THC content of their products.
