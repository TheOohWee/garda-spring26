For 2022, non-GAAP operating expenses were $160.4 million compared to $126.4 million for 2021. GAAP operating margin for Q4 was 45% and 44% for the full year of 2022.

Non-GAAP operating margin for the fourth quarter of 2022 was 50% and 49% for the full year 2022. Once again, we ended the quarter with a strong balance sheet.

As of December 31, 2022, the company had cash and cash equivalents, marketable securities and deposits of $547.4 million. This quarter, InMode generated $57.2 million from operating activities.
