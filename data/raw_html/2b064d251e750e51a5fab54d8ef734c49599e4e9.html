Earnings Call Transcript

On Holding (ONON) Q4 2024 Earnings Call Transcript

By Motley Fool Transcribing – Dec 23, 2025 at 11:04AM EST

Image source: The Motley Fool.

DATE

Tuesday, March 4, 2025 at 8 a.m. ET

CALL PARTICIPANTS

  • Co-Founder and Executive Board Member — David Allemann
  • Co-CEO and CFO — Martin Hoffmann
  • Co-CEO — Marc Maurer

Need a quote from a Motley Fool analyst? Email [email protected]

TAKEAWAYS

  • Net Sales -- CHF 2.32 billion for the year, up 33.2% on a constant currency basis.
  • Q4 Net Sales -- CHF 606.6 million, representing 40.6% growth on a constant currency basis.
  • Direct-to-Consumer (D2C) Share -- 48.8% in Q4 with D2C net sales of CHF 296.2 million, up 48.2% on a constant currency basis.
  • Wholesale Net Sales -- CHF 310.4 million in Q4, growing 34.2% on a constant currency basis.
  • Gross Profit Margin -- 62.1% in Q4, a 170 basis point increase year over year; full year gross margin reached 60.6%.
  • Adjusted EBITDA Margin -- 16.4% in Q4; 16.7% for the full year, up from 15.5% the prior year.
  • Net Income -- CHF 89.5 million in Q4, with a CHF 38 million foreign exchange gain.
  • Apparel Net Sales -- Over CHF 100 million in 2024, with Q4 apparel sales rising 77.5% to CHF 32.6 million.
  • Capital Expenditure -- 2.8% of net sales for 2024, up from 2.6% in 2023, driven by retail expansion.
  • Net Working Capital -- Improved to 21.5% of net sales in 2024 from 27.7% the previous year.
  • Operating Cash Flow -- CHF 510.6 million in 2024, more than doubling year-over-year.
  • Total Cash Position -- CHF 924.3 million at year end, up from CHF 494.6 million at the end of 2023.
  • APAC Performance -- Q4 net sales of CHF 74.1 million, up 124.6% on a constant currency basis; driven by Japan, China, and store openings in Hong Kong.
  • Americas Growth -- Q4 net sales of CHF 385.1 million, up 33.9% on a constant currency basis; Brazil more than doubled net sales, notably in apparel.
  • EMEA Growth -- Q4 net sales of CHF 147.4 million, up 33.1% on a constant currency basis; benefits from new stores in Paris and Milan.
  • Product Franchise Growth -- Cloudmonster, Cloudsurfer, and Cloudrunner grew between 60% and 140% during the year.
  • Younger Consumer Uptake -- Share of products sold to customers age 35 and younger increased by 6-8 percentage points for top franchises.
  • Retail Expansion -- Opened 19 new stores in 2024, extending presence into Europe, Americas, and Asia-Pacific.
  • Wholesale Door Growth -- 10,700 wholesale doors at year end with a plan to expand to approximately 11,300 in 2025.
  • 2025 Net Sales Outlook -- At least CHF 2.94 billion, representing at least 27% constant currency growth.
  • 2025 Gross Margin Guidance -- Around 60.5%, accounting for foreign exchange headwinds and D2C mix improvements.
  • 2025 Adjusted EBITDA Margin Guidance -- Expected to be in the range of 17%-17.5%.
  • Apparel Category Target -- Medium-term goal for apparel sales to reach 10% of total revenues.
  • China Retail Expansion -- 58 stores at year end (30 own retail, 28 franchise) with plans to grow to 80 in 2025, including new flagships in Chengdu and Shenzhen.

SUMMARY

On Holding AG (ONON 2.58%) delivered record quarterly growth in Q4 and achieved its highest full-year gross and EBITDA margins in company history. The company reported significant acceleration across all regions and product channels, originating from both D2C and wholesale strategies, and identified expanding brand awareness among younger consumers as a core driver. Management outlined clear multi-year financial targets, robust 2025 outlooks across major metrics, and emphasized enhanced operational discipline, indicating scalability through investments in retail, technology, and automation while reaffirming mid- and long-term category and geographic expansion plans.

  • Co-CEO Hoffmann said, "we expect our Q1 growth rate somewhere in the low to mid-30s." and further added that And currently, that growth is above what is implied in our guidance. But as I said, given what we are seeing -- on the macro side, we want to stay prudent here.
  • Management confirmed that elevated apparel growth was disproportionately driven by regions with strong owned retail presence and plans to streamline the assortment for efficiency gains.
  • Maintaining upper funnel marketing investments at 11%-12% of sales in 2025 is strategic for brand momentum, according to Hoffmann.
  • Store expansion in China is accelerating, with flagship locations and increased average store size set to provide greater point-of-sale reach and drive regional sales.
  • Hoffmann highlighted that, despite increased investment in technology and operations, leverage in G&A and improved distribution efficiency are expected to support operating margin targets in 2025.
  • Management stated, "strategy is being appreciated, and it allows us to not play in the field of where all the others are playing, but capture a different consumer with a higher margin profile."

INDUSTRY GLOSSARY

  • D2C: Direct-to-consumer sales channel in which products are sold directly to end customers through company-owned stores or online platforms, bypassing third-party retailers.
  • Cloud franchise: Distinct branded lines of athletic footwear (e.g., Cloudmonster, Cloudsurfer) introduced and owned by On Holding AG, each serving specific market segments or use cases within the sportswear category.
  • Upper funnel marketing: Brand-building and awareness-oriented marketing activities aimed at generating initial consumer interest, as opposed to direct conversion-focused tactics.
