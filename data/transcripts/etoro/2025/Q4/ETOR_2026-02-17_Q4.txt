Industry glossary
Net contribution
: Revenue remaining after direct variable costs, used to assess business line performance.
CopyTrader
: eToro's feature allowing users to replicate the trades of selected investors.
Smart portfolios
: Bundled, theme-based investment strategies managed by eToro or partners, accessible on the platform.
Adjusted EBITDA
: Earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, and amortization, adjusted for certain items to better reflect core profitability.
Take rate
: The proportion of transaction value retained as revenue from trading, important for assessing platform monetization.
Noncustodial wallet
: A crypto wallet where users control the private keys and are responsible for asset security, as opposed to custodial solutions managed by platforms.
ISA
: U.K. Individual Savings Account an account offering tax advantages for investments.
App store (eToro context)
: Planned hub for community-developed and eToro-developed applications, leveraging AI to deliver enhanced trading tools to users.
Tokenization
: Process of converting real-world or traditional assets into digital tokens on a blockchain.
