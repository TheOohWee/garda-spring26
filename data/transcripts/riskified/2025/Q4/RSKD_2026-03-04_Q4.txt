Support for ACH, crypto, stablecoins, fiat conversion, and stored value again, nothing comparable. On agentic checkout, we haven t seen anything comparable to our recent releases. Networks offer other services aimed at FIs tokenization, 3-D Secure rails which aren t our focus.
But in a Venn diagram, what we do is largely distinct.
