SUMMARY
The company highlighted a transition year marked by the realignment of its fleet and divestiture of low-return assets, directly resulting in $21 million of new liquidity and investment into higher-yield Capesize vessels. Management confirmed secured contracted earnings for both new Capesize assets Dukeship and Squareship at fixed daily rates through 2026, which increases earnings and cash flow transparency. Improved Panamax and Capesize market indices, along with disciplined OpEx controls and low fleet growth, underpin stable operational and financial expectations moving forward.
The announced exit from the offshore energy construction vessel yielded approximately 1.7 million in profit and a return on invested capital of approximately 15%.
The combined Capesize investments total approximately $62 million, supporting a fleet strategy concentrated on higher-earning asset classes.
Year-end liquidity guidance is for levels to normalize at approximately $2 million per vessel as new transactions settle.
Stavros Gyftakis stated, "the free cash flow of the vessel would be around 10 to 12,000 per day," referring to the Squareship vessel under its fixed charter arrangement.
The order book for new dry bulk vessels remains historically low, with over 30% of the global fleet aged beyond fifteen years.
INDUSTRY GLOSSARY
Time Charter Equivalent (TCE)
: Standardized measure of voyage revenue divided by available days, assessing vessel earning performance irrespective of charter arrangement.
OpEx
: Daily vessel operating expenses, excluding debt service, depreciation, and overhead.
Scrubber-Fitted
: Vessels equipped with exhaust gas cleaning systems to comply with IMO emissions regulations.
Bareboat Charter
: Contract in which a vessel is leased without crew or provisions, and operational expenses are borne by the charterer.
P/V (Purchase Option Value)
: Contracted price at which an asset may be acquired at the end of a lease or charter period.
LTV (Loan-to-Value)
: Metric expressing outstanding debt as a percentage of asset market value, assessing financial leverage risk.
