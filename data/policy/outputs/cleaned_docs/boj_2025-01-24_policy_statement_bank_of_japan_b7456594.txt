In conjunction with the above change in the monetary market adjustment policy, it was decided to change the applicable interest rates of various systems as follows. For the complementary lending facility, the applicable interest rate, the standard lending rate, will be set at 0.75%. Regarding the support for increased lending, it is planned to end new lending by the end of June 2025 as scheduled. During this time, the overseas economy has been on a gradual growth path, and while various uncertainties are recognized, the international financial capital markets are generally stable. In light of this situation, it was judged appropriate to adjust the degree of monetary easing from the perspective of achieving the 2% 'price stability target' sustainably and stably. Even after the change in the policy interest rate, real interest rates will remain significantly negative, and the accommodative financial environment will be maintained, so it is believed that economic activity will continue to be firmly supported. The new monetary market adjustment policy will take effect from the next business day (January 27). The applicable interest rates for the complementary deposit facility and the standard lending rate will take effect from the next business day (January 27).
