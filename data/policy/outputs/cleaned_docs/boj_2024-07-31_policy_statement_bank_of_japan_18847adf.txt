Regarding the plan to reduce long-term government bond purchases. In the monetary policy meeting in June next year, an interim evaluation of the plan to reduce long-term government bond purchases will be conducted. In the event of a rapid rise in long-term interest rates, regardless of the planned monthly purchase amount, we will flexibly implement increases in purchase amounts, fixed-rate operations, and common collateral funding operations. If necessary, the reduction plan may be reviewed in the monetary policy decision meeting.
