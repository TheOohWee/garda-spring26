The Governing Council confirms that it will discontinue the reinvestments under the asset purchase programme as of July 2023. The Governing Council will continue applying flexibility in reinvesting redemptions coming due in the PEPP portfolio. The Governing Council stands ready to adjust all of its instruments within its mandate to ensure that inflation returns to its 2% target over the medium term. The Governing Council will continue to follow a data-dependent approach to determining the appropriate level and duration of restriction. Indicators of underlying price pressures remain strong, although some show tentative signs of softening. Staff have revised up their projections for inflation excluding energy and food, especially for this year and next year. They now see it reaching 5.1% in 2023, before it declines to 3.0% in 2024 and 2.3% in 2025. The decline will amount to €15 billion per month on average until the end of June 2023.
