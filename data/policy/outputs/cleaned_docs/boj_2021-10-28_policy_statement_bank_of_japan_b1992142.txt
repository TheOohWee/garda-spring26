To ensure that the 10-year government bond yield remains around 0%, we will conduct purchases of long-term government bonds without setting an upper limit on the necessary amount. We will continue to strive for support for corporate financing and the stability of financial markets through (1) the special program for funding support in response to COVID-19, (2) ample supply of yen and foreign currency without setting upper limits through government bond purchases and dollar operations, and (3) purchases of ETFs and J-REITs with annual increase rates capped at approximately 12 trillion yen and 1.8 billion yen, respectively. For the time being, we will closely monitor the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic and, if necessary, will not hesitate to implement additional monetary easing measures. Regarding the policy interest rate, we expect it to remain at the current level of short- and long-term interest rates or below that level. Opposition: Member Kataoka. Member Kataoka opposed the decision, arguing that it would be desirable to strengthen monetary easing by lowering short- and long-term interest rates to support positive corporate investment in the post-COVID era.
