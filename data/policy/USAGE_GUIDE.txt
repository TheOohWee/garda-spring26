Main script: `policy_score.py`

Run from this folder:
`py -3 policy_score.py`

Inputs:
- `Data/` (source files)
- `raw_docs/` + `raw_docs_metadata.csv` are rebuilt automatically when `Data/` changes

Main outputs:
- `outputs/cleaned_documents.csv`
- `outputs/all_excerpts_dataset.csv`
- `outputs/scored_sentences.csv`
- `outputs/sample_scored_excerpts.csv`
- `outputs/regional_policy_scores.csv`
