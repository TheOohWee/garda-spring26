The Committee decided to maintain the target range for the federal funds rate at 5-1/4 to 5-1/2 percent. The Committee does not expect it will be appropriate to reduce the target range until it has gained greater confidence that inflation is moving sustainably toward 2 percent. In support of its goals, the Committee will continue reducing its holdings of Treasury securities and agency debt and agency mortgage-backed securities, as described in its previously announced plans. The Committee is strongly committed to returning inflation to its 2 percent objective. The Committee seeks to achieve maximum employment and inflation at the rate of 2 percent over the longer run.
