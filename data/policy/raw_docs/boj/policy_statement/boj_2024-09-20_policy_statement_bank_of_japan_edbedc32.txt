The Bank will encourage the uncollateralized overnight call rate to remain at around 0.25%. The financial environment in our country is in a relaxed state. In terms of prices, looking at the year-on-year change in consumer prices (excluding fresh food), the impact of price pass-through from past increases in import prices has been diminishing, but service prices have continued to rise gradually due to wage increases, resulting in a current level in the upper 2% range. The expected inflation rate is gradually rising. Looking at risk factors, uncertainties surrounding the economy and prices in our country remain high due to overseas economic and price trends, resource price movements, and corporate wage and price-setting behavior.
