To ensure that the 10-year government bond yield remains around 0%, we will conduct purchases of long-term government bonds without setting an upper limit on the necessary amount. In doing so, the interest rate may fluctuate to some extent depending on economic and price conditions. In the event of a rapid rise in interest rates, we will promptly and appropriately conduct bond purchases. Member Kataoka opposed, stating that it would be desirable to strengthen monetary easing by lowering short- and long-term interest rates in response to the increasing downward pressure on prices and to alleviate the interest burden on businesses and households.
