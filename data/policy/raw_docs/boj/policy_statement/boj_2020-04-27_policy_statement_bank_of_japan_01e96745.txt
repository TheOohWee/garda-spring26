It was judged appropriate to further strengthen monetary easing. The additional purchase limit for CP and corporate bonds will be significantly expanded, with a total upper limit of approximately 20 trillion yen for purchases. Expansion of the scope of eligible collateral to include household debt and all private sector debt. Further aggressive purchases of both long-term and short-term government bonds will be conducted. Purchases of long-term government bonds will be made without setting an upper limit to ensure that the 10-year government bond yield remains around zero percent. For the time being, we will closely monitor the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic and will not hesitate to implement additional monetary easing measures if necessary. In the event of a rapid rise in interest rates, government bond purchases will be implemented swiftly and appropriately. Relaxation of purchase limits for each issuer of CP and corporate bonds and the outline of new funding supply measures.
