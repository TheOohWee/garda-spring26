To ensure that the 10-year government bond yield remains around 0%, we will conduct purchases of long-term government bonds without setting an upper limit on the necessary amount. In doing so, the yield may fluctuate to some extent depending on economic and price conditions. We will continue to strive for support for corporate financing and the stability of financial markets through (1) the special program for funding support in response to COVID-19, (2) ample supply of yen and foreign currency without setting upper limits on government bond purchases and dollar operations, and (3) active purchases of ETFs and J-REITs. For the time being, we will closely monitor the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic and, if necessary, will not hesitate to implement additional monetary easing measures. In the event of a rapid rise in interest rates, we will promptly and appropriately conduct government bond purchases. As a basic policy for the purchase of ETFs and J-REITs, we will continue to conduct purchases to ensure that the holdings increase at a pace equivalent to approximately 6 trillion yen and 90 billion yen per year, respectively, and in doing so, the purchase amounts may fluctuate up and down depending on market conditions, with appropriate consideration for the impact on asset price premiums.
