To ensure that the 10-year government bond yield remains around 0%, we will conduct purchases of long-term government bonds without setting an upper limit on the necessary amount. We will continue to support corporate financing and maintain stability in the financial markets through (1) the special program for COVID-19 response funding support, (2) abundant supply of yen and foreign currency without setting upper limits on government bond purchases and dollar operations, and (3) purchases of ETFs and J-REITs at an annual increase pace of approximately 12 trillion yen and 1.8 billion yen, respectively. For the time being, we will closely monitor the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic and, if necessary, will not hesitate to implement additional monetary easing measures. Regarding the policy interest rate, we assume that it will remain at the current level of short- and long-term interest rates or below that level. Opposition: Member Kataoka. Member Kataoka opposed the decision, arguing that it would be desirable to strengthen monetary easing by lowering short- and long-term interest rates in response to the increasing downward pressure on prices and to support positive corporate investment in the post-COVID era.
