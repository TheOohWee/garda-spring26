The Bank will encourage the uncollateralized overnight call rate to remain at around 0.25%. In favor: Members Ueda, Himino, Uchida, Adachi, Nakamura, Noguchi, Nakagawa, Takada. Opposed: Member Tamura. Member Tamura submitted a proposal to encourage the uncollateralized overnight call rate to remain at around 0.5%, citing the increasing upside risk to prices as the economy and prices follow the outlook, which was rejected by a majority vote.
