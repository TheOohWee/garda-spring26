Today the Governing Council decided to keep the key ECB interest rates unchanged. We decided to increase the envelope of the pandemic emergency purchase programme (PEPP) by €500 billion to a total of €1,850 billion. We decided to extend the reinvestment of principal payments from maturing securities purchased under the PEPP until at least the end of 2023. We decided to further recalibrate the conditions of the third series of targeted longer-term refinancing operations (TLTRO III). We decided to extend to June 2022 the duration of the set of collateral easing measures adopted by the Governing Council. We decided to offer four additional pandemic emergency longer-term refinancing operations (PELTROs) in 2021. We decided to continue conducting our regular lending operations as fixed rate tender procedures with full allotment at the prevailing conditions for as long as necessary.
